AWS VPN Notes

  1. Virtual private gateway: A Virtual Private Gateway (also known as a VPN Gateway) is the endpoint on the AWS VPC side of your VPN connection.
  2. Customer Gateway: An AWS resource that provides information to AWS about your Customer Gateway device.
  3. Customer Gateway device: A physical device or software application on the customer side of the Site-to-Site VPN connection.
  4. VPN tunnel: An encrypted link where data can pass from the customer network to or from AWS.
